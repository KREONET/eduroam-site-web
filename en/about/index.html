About eduroam

Learn about the eduroam service at Sandbox Research Institute

What is eduroam?

eduroam (education roaming) is an international Wi-Fi roaming service for universities, research institutions, and public organizations. With a single institutional account, you can connect to Wi-Fi at any participating institution worldwide — no additional registration or fees required.

eduroam is active in more than 100 countries and thousands of institutions. In Korea, it is operated by KREONET(KISTI) through eduroamKR.

eduroam at Sandbox Research Institute

Sandbox Research Institute is a member of eduroamKR, providing Wi-Fi roaming service to its members both on-campus and at eduroam-enabled institutions worldwide.

  • On-site eduroam AP installation and operation
  • Member account-based authentication (802.1X / WPA2-Enterprise)
  • Domestic and international eduroam roaming support
  • eduroam CAT (Configuration Assistant Tool) available

Security & Authentication

eduroam uses WPA2-Enterprise (EAP) technology to encrypt user credentials. Authentication is handled by your home institution's RADIUS server — your password is never sent to any other institution.
